Kapil Agrawal

Biography

Hi, I am a CS Ph.D. student at UC Irvine. I am advised by Prof. Sangeetha Abdu Jyothi. My research focusses on improving the resilience of cloud infrastructure. Previously, I worked as a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India under the guidance of Dr. Venkat Padmanabhan and Dr. Sreangsu Acharyya on monitoring network availability in cloud-services like outlook, bing, and skype. I was also advised by Shwetabh Khanduja and Dr. Arun Iyer on the project AI4Work on building intelligent RL based agents for recommending user action items based on their preferences in enterprise settings.
I completed my graduation with a B. Tech. in Mathematics and Computing from Delhi Technological University in 2017. After graduating, I spent eight months working with Prof. Varun Dutt as a Research Assistant on building low cost MEMs based IoT devices for monitoring and early detection of landslides.

Research

My research interests are in cloud computing and software engineering with a special focus on large-scale failure mitigation and disaster management techniques for cloud infrastructure and cloud-scale services.

  • Phoenix, a cooperative graceful degradation solution for mitigating disasters in datacenter.
  • Multi-cloud Resilience an ongoing project to study
  • Storage Resilience
  • Some past work on landslide detection and alerting during my stint at IIT Mandi:
    • A comparison of class imbalance techniques for real-world landslide predictions
    • Minute-scale prediction of soil movement using machine-learning techniques
